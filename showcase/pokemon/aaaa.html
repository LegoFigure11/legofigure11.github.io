Oh boy, this Beldum... a ridiculous idea for a RS Egg RNG, my aim for this Pokémon was to get something that was uniquely me, and what better candidate for such a project than Beldum? I realised that "Lego" could be represented by the number 1390, and my mission became clear – I had to fill as many of this things internals with that number as possible.

That resulted in it ending up with both a TID and SID of 1390, a PID of 13901390, and the IVs listed above, hatched in Meteor Falls for style points. Normally, this wouldn't pose too much of an issue, except that VBA isn't completely accurate when it comes to RS/FRLG Breeding, making an extra RNG call in the middle of the second half of the generation which results in a strange mix between the normal and split method that at the time no tool supported searching for. Cue several days of pain and suffering as I try to figure out what is happening and why it's going wrong, before eventually writing my own script to try and generate the spread, only for it to return no results after about 10 hours of searching.

Eventually, I gave up on VBA and decided to try BizHawk instead, where I got it on the second attempt thanks to the lack of the extra RNG call. My calibration attempt on BizHawk resulted in a Beldum with the PID AAAA1390, and seeing how well that described what I was feeling having finally hit my target, I decided to name this Beldum "AAAAAAAAAA". Once I heard them shrieking in my boxes, I decided to revise it down to just "AAAA" so as not to disturb my other Pokémon too much.

This Beldum is still Lv. 5 and is fully Ribbon Mastered (despite how difficult the contests are with such a heavily restricted movepool) with thanks to Skadiv for helping out with Gen 8 as well as putting up with my complaining during every stage of both RNGing and ribboning this Beldum.
