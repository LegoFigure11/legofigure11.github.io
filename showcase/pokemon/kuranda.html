Kuranda is my first ever truly "complete" ribbon master with all 85 ribbons, including the National Ribbon from Colosseum/XD. While Butterfree might seem like an unusual choice for a Ribbon Master, I've had a soft spot for the Compound Eyes Sleep Powder–spamming butterflies ever since cant say sent me a Gen 6 team featuring River-pattern Vivillon that we both used with great success. He used another team featuring Vivillon to get out of a slump and in to a high ladder position during Gen 7, and when Vivillon fell victim to Dexit in Gen 8, he instead decided to build a team around Butterfree. I took to this team like a duck to water, and have used it or a variation of it every series since. As such, I decided to RNG and fully Ribbon Kuranda to pay homage to the oft-overlooked early game bugs that have triumphed over so many opponents across the last 3 generations.

The nickname Kuranda is a personal reference to a small town in Far North Queensland, Australia, nestled amongst tropical rainforest. It is home to a butterfly sanctuary that I was lucky enough to visit on a childhood holiday back in 2004 after seeing it on a travel television show. This Ribbon Master wouldn't have been possible without the help of Skadiv in Gen 4 and Gen 8, thank you for your assistance!
