Named after the famous Australian singer-songwriter, Kylie Minogre was an insane Ribbon Master project that I decided to undertake only a few days before boxart Legends got re-banned from SwSh online ranked battles. Everything about this big pink fish was a mad rush, from the Sapphire playthrough to catch it in the first place to the Gen 3 and 4 contests. Most of her journey through the early gens was streamed to the Smogon Wi-Fi discord server at the fastest speed my computer could handle.

Fortunately everything went without a hitch, and I was able to get the Master Rank ribbon on her on the final day of the season. After that I was feeling a bit burnt out so I took my time with the rest of the Gen 8 ribbons, but thanks to a little help from Skadiv she's now complete! She's available Lv. 45 "untouched" (well, as untouched as a Ribbon Master can be...), but I'll most likely train a BR copy for use on my Tower team. Should GameFreak ever bring back Primals, she absolutely will not be allowed to undergo Primal Reversion though because Primal Kyogre's purple fingies are a sin.
