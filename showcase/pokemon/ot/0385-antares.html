Jirachi has been one of my favourite Pokémon ever since I discovered it in the back of a PRIMA guide in 2005, where its stats and movepool were blanked out because it was so mysterious. Since then I've managed to get my hands on a few from events in my childhood and even RNG'd a shiny WISHMKR one, but with Channel being the "final frontier" of my GameCube RNG experience and the availability of far better shiny spreads, it only seemed fitting to get one from there as well.

Antares is one half of a pair of CHANNEL Jirachi, his sister being a non-shiny 6IV named "Alcyone" gifted to kasib. Both are named after stars (with thanks to kasib for inspiration) and are full Ribbon Masters as on Gen 8. This would not have been possible without the help of pilica12 with the Gen 4 Contest Ribbons and Max. Optimizer for renaming Antares from Betelgeuse in Gen 7 after I changed my mind and wanted him named after a bigger, redder star.
