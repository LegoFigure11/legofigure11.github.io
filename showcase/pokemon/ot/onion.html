I fell in love with Eldegoss the moment I saw it revealed, and after a close friend sent me some art of it done up as Miror B. from the Orre games I quickly adopted it as my mascot and just knew I had to have one. But not just any old Eldegoss would do, no, it had to be special – so I decided to hunt for a shiny with a specific personality mark using the one-time encounter on the Route 5 bridge. After 21 revisions of a bot written by Anubis and 227,967 encounters over the span of several months, the perfect Eldegoss finally showed up and bonked me on the head!

His nickname is born of me changing my name to "Mother Onion" in response to a meme posted in the Smogon Wi-Fi discord server, and because I was using the Miror B. Eldegoss as my profile picture we quickly concluded that Eldegoss was not in fact cotton but rather an onion. The name stuck, and the rest is history! Onion has all the available ribbons as of Gen 8, and sees use on my BSS team alongside Kuranda thanks to cant say's teambuilding genius.
