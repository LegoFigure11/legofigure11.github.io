This Snorunt is my proudest ever Retail RNG, a flawless shiny breed abused on my Emerald cart in 2016 while I was at school, with my GBA hidden in my backpack. I chose Naive nature because I wanted to use the recently-released Mega Glalie with Double-Edge and Freeze Dry, and Double-Edge was only available as a Gen 3 tutor.

She's named after my Discord bot, moodE, who originated as a bot for the Battle Spot room on Pokémon Showdown! and was named by cant say and NOVED as a portmanteau of Glalie's Ability Moody (with which it was extremely dominant in BSS at the time), and the suffix –E, a customary ending to the name of many PS! bots at the time.

She has grown up through the years just as my bot has, and was the first ever Ribbon Master that I completed with a little help from Skadiv in Gen 8. Fortunately, thanks to the Ability Patch, she can finally have the ability Moody and fulfill her destiny!

While she has all of the ribbons at Lv. 5, there is also a Lv. 100 fully BR evolved version of her with the Battle Ready mark and the Master Ball-tier Ribbon from Gen 8.
