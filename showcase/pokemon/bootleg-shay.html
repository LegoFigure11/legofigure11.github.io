Bootleg Shaymin was caught as a very lucky wild encounter during the 2021 "Spring into Spring" event, while hunting for Shiny Flower Crown Eevee. She is so named because in efforts to be more like the small cabbage we all know and love, she is round, green, and wears pink flowers on her head. I like to imagine that sometimes she replaces the egg in her pouch with a little Shaymin plushie and carries it around with her, too.

She was Lucky Traded to me, where she has since earned the Best Buddy ribbon and makes a great adventuring partner. By some freak luck, her IVs allow her to reach exactly 1390CP at Lv. 50, the maximum level for Pokémon in GO. Because she has a cute flower hat she is stuck in Pokémon GO forever but I wouldn't have it any other way.
